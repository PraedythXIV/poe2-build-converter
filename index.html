Passive tree

A read-only view of your build's passive tree, exactly as imported — hover any node for its stats, jewels and tooltips, search to jump to a node, and pan/zoom to explore. The tree is not editable here: your .build always exports the tree from your PoB code, unchanged.

Variants

One PoB can hold several loadouts. The rows below are auto-detected from your PoB's loadouts — each becomes its own .build. Review them and fix anything mis-detected: rename, reorder, add, remove, or re-map the tree/skills/gear. The Convert step downloads them all (one file, or several).

Publish to PoE2: upload your downloaded .build at pathofexile2.com/my-account/builds — players who subscribe to it get the build auto-loaded in-game.

Atlas tree planner

Planning only: click nodes to allocate them (auto-pathing from the atlas roots) and share the result with Copy plan link. The in-game Build Planner format has no atlas fields, so an atlas plan still can't be exported to a .build.

Genesis tree planner

Planning only: the Genesis Tree is five independent crafting sub-trees (Currency, Rings, Amulets, Belts, Breachstones). Click nodes to allocate them (each sub-tree paths from its own womb start) and share the result with Copy plan link. Like the atlas, a Genesis plan can't be exported to a .build — the format has no Genesis fields.
